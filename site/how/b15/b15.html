Wildlife, like aquatic life, is very important, so we must protect it, and if life on land disappears, we will not be able to live, eat or breathe

LIFE ON LAND

Goal 15: The nine “target outcomes” include: conservation and restoration of terrestrial and freshwater ecosystems; halting deforestation and restoring degraded forests; combating desertification and restoring degraded lands; ensuring the conservation of mountain ecosystems, the protection of biodiversity and natural habitats; protecting access to genetic resources and the equitable sharing of their benefits; stopping poaching and trafficking in protected species; preventing the introduction of invasive alien species into terrestrial and aquatic ecosystems; and integrating the values ​​of ecosystems and biodiversity into government planning.

THE TARGET

"We should Protecting and restoring terrestrial ecosystems and promoting their sustainable use, managing forests in a sustainable manner, and we must not cut down and burn trees because this harms the environment, combat desertification, halt and reverse land degradation, and stop the loss of biodiversity
