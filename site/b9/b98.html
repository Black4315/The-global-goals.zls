INDUSTRY INNOVATION AND INFRASTRUCTURE

Goal 9:Sustainable Development Goal 9 has eight targets, and progress is measured through twelve indicators. The first five goals are “results goals”: ​​to develop sustainable, resilient and inclusive infrastructure; promoting inclusive and sustainable industrial transformation; increasing access to financial services and markets; Developing all industries and infrastructures to become sustainable; Improving research and developing industrial technologies. The remaining three goals are considered “means of achievement”: facilitating the sustainable development of infrastructure in developing countries; Supporting the local development of technology and diversification of industries; Universal access to information and communication technology.
