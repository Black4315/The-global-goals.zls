GOOD HEALTH AND WELL-BEING

Goal 3:The third sustainable development goal seeks to achieve universal health coverage, which seeks to achieve equal access to health care services for all men and women. It is proposed to put an end to the preventable deaths of newborns, infants and children under five years of age (child deaths) and to end epidemics. Good health is fundamental to sustainable development and the 2030 Agenda. Goal 3 focuses on broader economic and social inequalities, urbanization, the climate crisis, and the persistent burden of HIV and other infectious diseases, without losing sight of emerging challenges such as noncommunicable diseases. Given the global COVID-19 pandemic, great attention must be paid to achieving good health and well-being on a global scale.."

THE TARGET

All people must exercise and eat healthy food. The state must ban drugs and prohibited substances because they harm human health. Health is the most important thing.
