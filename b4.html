QUALITY EDUCATION

Goal 4: aims to provide children and adolescents with quality and accessible education as well as other educational opportunities. One of its aims is the complete eradication of illiteracy, whether in reading, writing or arithmetic. An important component of acquiring valuable knowledge and skills is the learning environment. Hence the urgent need to build more educational facilities and upgrade existing facilities to provide a safe, effective and inclusive learning environment for all.

THE TARGET
